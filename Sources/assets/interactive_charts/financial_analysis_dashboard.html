The €1.5 Trillion Paradox: Italian Household Financial Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of Italian household financial behaviors compared to EU averages

SDA Bocconi School of Management
Analysis Date: April 23, 2025

Key Financial Analysis Insights:


The €1.5 Trillion Paradox:

Italian households hold €1577.4 billion in cash and deposits, which is 1.3x more relative to insurance products than the EU average.


Financial Impact:

With 5% annual inflation, this cash preference results in a value erosion of approximately €78.9 billion annually.


Insurance Gap:

Insurance comprises only 19.0% of total financial assets in Italy compared to 27.0% in the EU - a 29.6% difference.


Recommendations:

1. Target financial education initiatives highlighting inflation risks

2. Develop transparent, low-complexity insurance products

3. Focus on digital solutions that balance liquidity and protection
