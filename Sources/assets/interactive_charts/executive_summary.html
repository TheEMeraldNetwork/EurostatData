Executive Summary: The €1.5 Trillion Italian Financial Paradox

SDA Bocconi School of Management
Analysis Date: April 23, 2025

Key Finding

Italian households hold €1577.4 billion in cash and deposits, with a significantly higher cash-to-insurance ratio (150.2%) than the EU average (116.0%). This preference for cash results in approximately €78.9 billion in annual value erosion due to inflation.

Recommendations:

  1. Target financial education initiatives highlighting inflation risks
  2. Develop transparent, low-complexity insurance products
  3. Focus on digital solutions that balance liquidity and protection
